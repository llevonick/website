About Me

I am a junior at Tufts University who is currently pursuing a major in Computer Science. These responsibilities have improved my organizational skills and, as I have helped more students I have grown to become a better coder and teacher. In my free time I like to teach myself new coding languages, play games that have rich mechanics and storylines, and draw!
